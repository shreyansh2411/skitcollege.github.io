    Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Management & Gramothan (SKIT) inspired from the learnings of Swami Keshvanand, was established in the year 2000 by Technocrats and Managers Society for Advanced Learning. Today the institute is recognized as one of the centers of academic excellence in Northern India. The Institute is affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota for offering Postgraduate and Graduate Courses in Engineering and Management. Our sister institution Swami Keshvanand Institute of Pharmacy (SKIP) is affiliated to Rajasthan University of Health Sciences for offering Graduate Course in Pharmacy.
